About the Author

Hello! My name is Emily, and I am the heart behind The Poet's Cottage. I started this space as a way to share my love for writing and to create a cozy corner of the internet for fellow poets.

My poetry is often inspired by nature, quiet moments, and the simple beauty of everyday life. I believe words have a way of capturing the soul of a moment, and my goal is to share that with you. Thank you for visiting my cottage!

My Inspirations

  • Long walks in the forest
  • The sound of rain on a tin roof
  • Old books and forgotten stories
  • Playing with my two dogs
